Technologies

EMMA AI is a powerful software that helps enterprises monitor the well-being of their employees, by keeping track of their whereabout, their stress level and their body temp. And by keeping every employees insight, EMMA AI ensures a safe and sercure work environment. With the QuickLearn technology, the software will automatically sort the database based on Department, Age Group, Gender, etc... You can also assign a name to a particular face ID and pinpoint its location and look up its history in just one click. Work out of the box with modern nVIDIA GPU.
