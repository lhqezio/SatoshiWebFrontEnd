About us

Satori is software company founded in 2009 by a group of talented engineers with a belief that AI is the future, our mission is to inovate the AI industry, helping VFX artists and large scale entreprises.

Launched in 2014, we unveiled to the world emmaAI a powerful AI that helps enterprise takes care of their employees by keep track and monitor all abnormal behaviours to prevent unwanted situation to happen at the work place. From the knowledges we gained, we released a top of the line, extremely powerful tool for VFX artists max3D. In 2017, Satori expanded its product line with a camera with Lidar scaner, 128mps sensor and precision tracking that meant to be use with emma. We also have 2 more products waiting to be unveil on 3 March 2022. Don't miss it.

And this is just the start, while our products isn't necessary afforable yet, we're working tirelessly to make the products more affordable to more and more people.
